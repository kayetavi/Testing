About This Dashboard

The Damage Mechanism Dashboard is a specialized web-based tool for professionals in oil & gas, refinery, petrochemical, and chemical industries. It is based on standards and helps in identifying, evaluating, and understanding various damage mechanisms affecting fixed equipment, Damage Mechanism Corrosion Rate, Corrosion Diagram,Inventory.
